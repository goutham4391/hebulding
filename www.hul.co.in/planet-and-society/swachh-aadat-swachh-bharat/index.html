Swachh Aadat, Swachh Bharat

Haath Munh Bum, Bimari Hogi Kam

India faces immense challenges in the areas of water, sanitation and hygiene. Through our Haath Munh aur Bum initiative, we encourage people to adopt and teach 3 ‘Swachh Aadats’ that will go a long way in promoting good health and hygiene.

Now that you’re here, let us take you through a journey.

Dekho > Socho > Badlo

Dekho – Share our film ‘Playing Billion’ to spread awareness

Millions of children in our country are deprived of the simple joys of childhood like playing cricket, every day. The reason being repeated illness due to lack of basic sanitation and hygiene practices.
